About Me

What's this all about?
To be perfectly honest with you, I'm not entirely sure. I have a lot of ideas and documents floating around and I thought it would be fun to have them all in one place. This should also serve as me "academic e-portfolio," since that's something the youngins are looking for these days.
Why the retro template?
I copied this template from that of Fourmilab, the website of the late John Walker, founder of Autodesk, Inc., a company best known for its flagship product, AutoCAD. His website serves as an inspiration for my own and I hope to respect his memory with this homage.
