Courses

The following courses represent a full curriculum intended to bring a bright high schooler to the level of a master's student in physics. These courses are not intended to substitute a good textbook, but should be used as a pedagogical tool to help the curious reader in understanding the material.

Classical Mechanics

Classical mechanics describes the motions of bodies under the influence of forces as understood prior to the revolutionary developments of the twentieth century. Although rendered obsolete on the microscopic scale by quantum mechanics and relativity, classical mechanics remains the most accurate simple description on energy scales relevant to us humans.
